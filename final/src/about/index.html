Our Story

Founded by a team of passionate explorers and travel enthusiasts, Nicaragua Adventure was born from a deep love for this beautiful country's rich culture and stunning landscapes. Our founders, each with extensive backgrounds in Central American tourism and adventure travel, envisioned a company that could offer unique, immersive experiences in locations rich with history and natural beauty. From the colonial streets of Granada to the volcanic landscapes of Ometepe Island and the pristine beaches of the Corn Islands, our curated travel packages are designed to provide both adventure and relaxation.
